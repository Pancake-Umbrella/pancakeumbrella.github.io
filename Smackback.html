Smackback

Smackback is an epic platform fighter for up to 4 players online.

Be the next Star-Champion in this captivating alien TV-show by mastering the powers of several mythical beings unearthed by studying the ancient human history.

The stage is alive! Every round there is a different twist hindering or improving certain abilities.

Invite friends to best each other and find out who's the ultimate Star.
