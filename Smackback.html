Smackback

Smackback is an epic platform fighter for up to 4 players online.

Be the next Star-Champion in this captivating alien TV-show by mastering the powers of several mythical beings unearthed by studying the ancient human history.

The stage is alive! Every round there is a different twist hindering or improving certain abilities.

Invite friends to best each other and find out who's the ultimate Star.

Darkness

The Screen turns Black because the lights go out.

Luckily we can still see all the VFX which illuminates the environment in a different way.

Shmatrick

A simple-minded, slow alien guy.

He is always hungry.
In fact, he even got his job, when he was searching for a snack in the stadium and somehow ended up in the commentators’ booth.

He likes the other commentator. He sees him as a good friend, with whom he does some fun banter.

Moderators in their standard mood

Shmaddaeus

A nerdy, octopus-looking guy.

He wants to show that he’s very professional and always knows the best move or way to fight.

He always tries to make everything very proper, and if things don’t fit his own standard, he gets angry quickly.

He doesn’t really like the other guy. He thinks it’s a disgrace they're both in the same seat.

He often tries to speak down on the other commentator and gets at them with some witty comment, but they doesn’t seem to care, or even understand.
